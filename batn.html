OVERVIEW

As technology becomes more advanced in tracking our lives, we are offered with plethora of trackers and fitness devices that intend to improve our wellness. More often than not, the data provided and services offered are overwhelming and impersonal.

Taking a step back, we ask ourselves, what does wellness mean to us? Wellness is defined differently as we all have individual needs and goals in achieving better health. The Baton is a tool that motivates one’s wellness through social integration and flexibility.

INTERVIEW PARTICIPANTS

Wellness is a broad topic, thus participants were casually chosen to get a general idea of how individuals defined wellness and how it impacts their lives.

UNDERSTANDING OF THE SITUATION

Participants are conscious about wellness and tries to work hard at it. Most participants are aware what they should do to improve for better health. Unfortunately work and other various factors constantly gets in the way of care.
Focusing participants who do constantly make attempts in wellness is then studied through the means of motivation. In attempts for better insight, participant data is recategorized by motivational classification.

MOTIVATION ANALYSIS

Factors of motivation influencers and deterrents were broken down in determining significant relationships. Comparing various factors revealed that something that helps you motivate could also in turn discourage one’s wellness.

WAYS OF MOTIVATION

Several methods in encouraging wellness motivation was explored. Social interaction, personalization, and control were aspects that touched on many of participants motivation.

LOW FIDELITY BEHAVIOR EXPERIMENT

The Baton experience was tested to get a better understanding of user behavior. Partnership interaction was tested for about a week in determining the effectiveness of social motivation through simple text messages.

PHYSICAL DESIGN CONSIDERATIONS

Various forms for the Baton have been tested. Series of rapid prototyping testing yielded following desired characteristics:
lightweight, thin, unobtrusive, grip-focused texture, aesthetically pleasing, and solid material feel.
