OVERVIEW

Rice University Bioscience Research Collaborative serves as a facility for innovative research approach. Aside from its usual lab facilities, BRCâ€™s focal objective is to promote collaborative efforts between researchers of varied discipline from Rice University and Texas Medical Center. The team was asked to figure out how to configure a space to stimulate such collaboration with many constraints that is required of research facilities.

DESIGN FOR COLLABORATION

This collaboration is emphasized through its cylindrical "collaborative hub," which functions as gathering place for its tenants. The hub, containing double height spaces, has been carefully laid out to promote interaction for intellectual and social exchange.

PROGRAM FINESSE

With a collaborative hub concept in place, various schemes were studied for the optimal position of such program in context to other required spaces needed for the facility. Context was consider as well in respect to Rice University's architectural language and the medical district that the project faced.
