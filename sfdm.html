CONTEXTUAL STUDIES

Such detailed model provides precise site studies and analysis for any projects located in San Francisco. Some of the studies conducted were: shadow studies, wind analysis, skyline/view impacts, etc.

DATA REPRESENTATION

City data can be represented visually such as transit, political, historic, seismic, zoning, etc. Various representation exists today, but never in three dimensions with buildings. The digital model provides not only a different method in depicting data, but new insights in how we visualize context.

VISUALIZATION

The digital model provides a compelling presentation material for individual projects through 3D renders, diagrams and 3D prints. Using a more accurately represented city model helps its users to better visualize and understand the impact that a project would have from street level to overall city skyline.

MODEL CONSTRUCTION

The digital model is built on an existing GIS survey and public city lot information. Matching the given information with aerial views, photos, and building data report , a complete information is researched and utilized to build a functional accurate city model.
